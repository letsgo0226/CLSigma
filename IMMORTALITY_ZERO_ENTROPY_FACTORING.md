## Shannon-style zero entropy

For a finite distribution `X`:

```text
H_Shannon(X) = - sum_x P(x) log P(x)
```

A Shannon zero-entropy condition occurs when the model distribution is degenerate:

```text
exists x* such that P(x*)=1
and for all x != x*, P(x)=0
```

Inside CLSigma, this is represented by the finite residual:

with:

